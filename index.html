Why synthetic data

Real datasets have limits. Synthetic ones don't.

Infinite scenarios

Rain, night, occlusion, unusual angles — generate the rare edge cases real datasets almost never capture.

Zero privacy risk

No real people, faces, or private footage collected — no consent chains, storage risk, or compliance headache.

Pixel-perfect labels

Every generated object already has exact, machine-perfect annotations — no manual labeling pass required.

Edge-ready efficiency

Models are compressed and deployed directly on-device — cutting cloud GPU cost and energy use at the source.

The platform

One pipeline, from pixels to production hardware.

01

Generate

Synthesize labeled scenes for your exact use case — any object, any environment, any lighting.

02

Train

Fine-tune open-vocabulary detection models directly on the synthetic set — no manual labeling.

03
